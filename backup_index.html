Ragavi's TicTacToe Game

How to play - The aim of this game is to get 3 of your symbols in a row. You will start out with either being x or o with you opponent being the other. If you are x you will start and you will click one one the 9 squares avaliable. Once a square is clicked you will be unable to click it again. You will then take turns with your opponent clicking different squares until all sqaures have been clicked. If you happen to get 3 of your symbols in a row before your opponent you win and vice versa. If neither you or your opponent have won by the time all boxes have been clicked then the game is a draw.
